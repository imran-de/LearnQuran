What We Do

Thanks To Get Started With Us

Noorani Qaida with Tajweed

This class is designed to teach you the fundamentals of Quranic word formation from the ground up. The Noorani Qaida, which is an Arabic alphabet book, serves as the foundation for your learning. You will master the correct pronunciation and articulation of each letter and word, enabling you to read the Quran independently. The course is structured in an interactive manner to ensure a fast and effective learning experience.

Read More
Reading Quran with Tajweed

Tajweed classes are designed to teach you how to read the Quran with proper pronunciation and melody. You will learn the correct places to elongate words, adjust your tone to a deeper or lighter voice, and apply the appropriate rise and fall in pitch. This ensures that you recite the Quran in a harmonious and beautiful manner, in accordance with its intended style.

Read More
Learn Quran with Translation

This course is designed for students who wish to understand the meanings of Quranic verses in their native language and gain insight into what Allah conveys in the Quran. You will not only learn the true essence of the Quran but also how to apply its teachings in your daily life. The course is engaging and thought-provoking, encouraging deep reflection.

Read More
Online Islamic Studies

Learn about Islam in our accessible online class, available for you to join at your convenience. The course covers Islamic beliefs, the dos and don’ts in Islam, and key values such as honoring parents, speaking the truth, and treating others with kindness. It’s designed for anyone who wishes to gain knowledge and understanding of these fundamental teachings.
