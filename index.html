What I Do

Thanks To Get Started With Me

Al Qaidah An Nooraniyah With Tajweed

This class is designed to teach you the fundamentals of Quranic word formation from the ground up. The Noorani Qaida, which is an Arabic alphabet book, serves as the foundation for your learning. You will master the correct pronunciation and articulation of each letter and word, enabling you to read the Quran independently. The course is structured in an interactive manner to ensure a fast and effective learning experience.

Read More
Reading Quran with Tajweed

My Tajweed classes are designed to teach you how to read the Quran with proper pronunciation. You will learn the correct articulation of each letter and apply the rules of tajweed for accurate and clear recitation.

Read More
Learn Quran with Translation

This course is designed for students who want to understand the meanings of Quranic verses with clear English translations. As I teach, I provide detailed explanations to help you grasp the messages Allah conveys in the Quran. My approach encourages reflection and deeper understanding of the Quran’s teachings.

Read More
Online Islamic Studies

Learn about Islam in my accessible online class, available for you to join at your convenience. The course covers essential Islamic beliefs, practices, and teachings, providing a clear understanding of the core principles of Islam. It's designed for anyone who wishes to deepen their knowledge and understanding of the faith.
